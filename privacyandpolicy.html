3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Services: Deliver the business intelligence, contacts, and other information available on our platform.
  • Improve the Site and Services: Continuously monitor and enhance the quality of our platform.
  • Customer Support and Communication: Respond to your queries, provide customer service, and send you updates about our Services.
